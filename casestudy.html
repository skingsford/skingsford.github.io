Excelsior Tooling Co. is an Australian company that manufactures and markets a range of bespoke modular 50s-style mechanics cabinets and exports them globally. BRANDesign was fortunate to be consulted on the development of the Excelsior concept, almost from the moment of its inception.

BRANDesign created the visual identity, technical drawings and 3D product prototypes, then went on to design the marketing collateral including product placement illustrations, website design and development and even signage, garment embroidery design and 3D print preparation.

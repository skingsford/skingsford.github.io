Capital Transport

Capital Transport required an update of their existing brand that was cleaner and stronger than the encumbent design and that could be reproduced across all media channels.

BRANDesign started with the existing design and recreated it as a true 3D model whose colour and scale could be infinitely adjusted to suit any medium and device.

A library of artwork accompanied the style guide to ensure their brand appeared correctly in every situation.

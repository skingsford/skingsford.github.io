Tenix Defence - LHD Vessel

BRANDesign was commissioned by Tenix Defence Australia (since acquired by BAE Systems Australia) to create visualisations of the LHD (Landing Helicopter Dock) Aircraft Carrier currently under construction at Williamstown, Victoria. The $11.7b bid was a joint venture with Navantia of Spain, undertaken for the Australian Navy.

BRANDesign developed a comprehensive set of illustrations of the vessels in mission mode as well as internals such as the berthing dock and staff quarters.

The illustrations were developed for the executive summary of the successful bid and have now featured in a wide range of publications globally.
